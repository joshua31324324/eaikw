# Job Club Brand Guide

## Logo

- **Style:** Wordmark “Job Club” in bold sans‑serif type.
- **Grid Alignment:** Logo sits on a modular grid, ensuring balance and proportion.
- **Symbol (optional):** Abstract geometric mark (e.g., square or node cluster) to represent AI networks.
- **Usage:** Always left‑aligned, with consistent spacing from navigation and content blocks.
- **Restrictions:** No gradients, shadows, or decorative flourishes — maintain flat, minimal presentation.

## Colors

- **Primary:** Deep Blue (#1E3A8A) — trust, professionalism.
- **Secondary:** Bright Green (#10B981) — growth, energy.
- **Accent:** Warm Yellow (#FBBF24) — optimism, highlights.
- **Neutral:** Gray (#6B7280) — accessibility‑friendly text contrast.
- **Swiss Design Principle:** Use solid color fields, high contrast, and minimal palette. Apply color sparingly to emphasize hierarchy and function.

## Typography

- **Primary Typeface:** Helvetica Neue or Inter — sans‑serif, clean, grid‑friendly.
- **Headings:** Bold, uppercase or sentence case, aligned to grid.
- **Body Text:** Regular weight, left‑aligned, ragged right (no justification).
- **Code/Technical Snippets:** Monospace (e.g., Source Code Pro).
- **Swiss Design Principle:** Typography is the design — clear hierarchy, no ornamentation, functional readability.

## Voice & Tone

- **Professional yet approachable:** Speak directly, avoid jargon.
- **Encouraging and supportive:** Emphasize growth and opportunity.
- **Clear and actionable:** Step‑by‑step guidance, concise instructions.
- **Swiss Design Principle:** Content is objective, precise, and functional. Tone reflects clarity and neutrality.

## Component and Button Styles

- **Buttons:**
  - Rectangular, grid‑aligned.
  - Primary button: Deep Blue background, white text.
  - Secondary button: Outline style with Bright Green border.
  - Hover state: Accent Yellow highlight.
- **Cards:**
  - Flat design, no shadows.
  - Grid‑aligned with consistent spacing.
  - Title, short description, CTA.
- **Forms:**
  - Accessible labels, ARIA support.
  - Clear error states in Bright Green or Red (#DC2626).
- **Navigation:**
  - Sticky top nav, grid‑aligned.
  - Dropdowns for Events and Resources.
- **Checklist Component:**
  - Dynamic list with completed/missing asset indicators.
  - Grid‑aligned icons (checkmark, warning).

---

## Swiss Design Alignment

- **Grid System:** All components align to a modular grid for consistency.
- **Minimalism:** No decorative elements; focus on typography, spacing, and color hierarchy.
- **Functionality:** Every element serves a clear purpose — no superfluous design.
- **Accessibility:** High contrast, semantic markup, WCAG compliance.
