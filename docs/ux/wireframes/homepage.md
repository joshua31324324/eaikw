# Homepage Wireframe – Job Club

## Header

- **Logo:** Top-left wordmark “Job Club”.
- **Navigation:** Home | Onboarding | Events | Resources | Portfolio | Privacy.
- **Accessibility:** High contrast, skip-to-content link, keyboard focus visible.

## Hero Section

- **Headline:** Job Club — AI career accelerator.
- **Subheadline:** Helping NJIT students become AI consultants, AI startup founders, AI‑savvy developers, and portfolio‑ready job candidates.
- **Primary CTA:** Join Job Club / Start onboarding.
- **Secondary CTA:** Explore events.

## Mission Statement

- **Mission:** Turn students into career‑ready AI professionals with a guided, automated onboarding experience.
- **Supporting points:**
  - Onboards students into a structured career pipeline.
  - Connects to Discord, events, mentoring, and learning resources.
  - Guides setup of LinkedIn, GitHub, personal site, and Calendly.
  - Automates administrative workflows and tracks users in a CRM.
  - Meets accessibility, SEO, analytics, and GDPR standards.
- **CTA:** Learn how Job Club works.

## Upcoming Events Preview

- **Section title:** Upcoming events.
- **Event cards:** Title, date/time, short description, CTA [View details].
- **Actions:** See all events.

## Career Resources Highlights

- **Section title:** Career resources.
- **Resource cards:**
  - Optimize LinkedIn for AI jobs.
  - AI consulting portfolio starter guide.
  - GitHub profile that gets interviews.
- **Actions:** View all resources.

## Portfolio Showcase

- **Section title:** Model portfolio.
- **Preview:** Screenshot or embedded example of a student portfolio.
- **CTA:** Build your portfolio.

## Community and Integrations

- **Section title:** Join the community.
- **Discord:** Invite button [Join Discord], links to #jobclub‑intros, #events, #resources.
- **CRM mention:** HubSpot/Airtable/Notion integration for member tracking.
- **Automation:** Highlight onboarding email and checklist automation.

## Footer

- **Links:** Privacy policy, accessibility, contact.
- **GDPR:** Cookie banner with Accept, Reject, Preferences; analytics load only after consent.
- **Accessibility:** ARIA labels, semantic landmarks, keyboard navigation confirmed.
