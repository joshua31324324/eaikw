## Stage 1: AWARENESS (Week 1)

### What's Happening
Student discovers Job Club through word-of-mouth, social media, or class announcement.

### Student Mindset
- "What is this Job Club thing?"
- "Is this for me?"
- "What's the commitment?"
- "Will it actually help my career?"

### Key Touchpoints
| Touchpoint | Content | Action |
|-----------|---------|--------|
| **Discord announcement** | "Join Job Club!" | Student joins Discord |
| **Friend recommendation** | "You should check out Job Club" | Student asks friend for details |
| **Class announcement** | Prof mentions Job Club | Curious but no immediate action |
| **Instagram/Social** | Event announcement | Student views content |
| **Email invite** | "Start your AI career" | Student considers clicking |

### Emotions
- **Curious** - "Interesting, tell me more"
- **Skeptical** - "Is this legit?"
- **Overwhelmed** - "Another thing to join?"

### Key Messaging Needed
- **Social proof:** "200+ NJIT students in Job Club"
- **Clarity:** "6 months to career-ready"
- **Low friction:** "Free to join"
- **Urgency:** "Applications accepted through [date]"

### Success Metric
âœ… Student visits Job Club home page or clicks email link

## Stage 2: ONBOARDING (Week 2-4)

### What's Happening
Student completes onboarding form, sets goals, and gets personalized learning plan.

### Student Mindset
- "OK, let me try this"
- "What path is right for me?"
- "How much time will this take?"
- "What do I actually need to learn?"

### Key Touchpoints

#### 2A: DISCOVERY
| Touchpoint | Content | Action |
|-----------|---------|--------|
| **Home page** | Overview of 4 pillars | Reads about program |
| **About page** | Mission and team | Understands values |
| **Discord intro** | Welcome message | Introduces self |
| **First event** | Kickoff event or office hours | Meets other students |

#### 2B: ASSESSMENT
| Touchpoint | Content | Action |
|-----------|---------|--------|
| **Onboarding form** | 15-30 min questionnaire | Completes form |
| **Skill assessment** | "Rate your Python skills" | Honest self-assessment |
| **Goal setting** | "What's your target role?" | Thinks about future |
| **Time availability** | "How many hours/week?" | Realistic commitment |

#### 2C: PERSONALIZATION
| Touchpoint | Content | Action |
|-----------|---------|--------|
| **Learning path** | Recommended curriculum | Sees custom plan |
| **Welcome email** | "Here's your path" | Gets actionable next steps |
| **Resource recommendations** | Curated starting resources | Bookmarks key guides |
| **Mentor matching** | "Your mentor is..." (optional) | Meets mentor if available |

### Emotions
- **Hope** - "This might actually work"
- **Relief** - "Someone tells me what to do"
- **Motivation** - "I can do this"
- **Anxiety** - "What if it's too hard?"

### Friction Points & Solutions

| Friction | Pain | Solution |
|---------|------|----------|
| **Form too long** | "I don't have 30 min" | Offer quick 10-min path |
| **Too technical** | "I don't understand the questions" | Simplify language, add help text |
| **Unclear benefit** | "Why are they asking this?" | Add context: "This helps us personalize your path" |
| **Commitment anxiety** | "What if I fail?" | Show examples of past students |
| **No immediate action** | "Where do I start?" | One clear next step highlighted |

### Success Metrics
âœ… Completes onboarding form
âœ… Receives personalized learning path
âœ… Bookmarks first 3 resources
âœ… Joins Discord channel

## Key Insights

1. **Personalization is critical** - Paths differ significantly by student type
2. **Community matters most** - Peer support drives retention
3. **Early momentum builds confidence** - First event attendance is crucial
4. **Tangible progress motivates** - Portfolio and projects > abstract learning
5. **Mentorship creates loyalty** - Regular 1:1 contact increases commitment
6. **Celebration drives engagement** - Public wins and milestones matter
7. **Flexibility is essential** - Working students need options
8. **Alumni become advocates** - Success students recruit peers
