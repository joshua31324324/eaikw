# Problem Statement & Goals

## Audience

NJIT seniors from non-technical majors who want to switch into AI careers before graduation.

## Problem Statement

Career-switching seniors are motivated but face barriers:  

- Limited technical background compared to peers.  
- Lack of confidence in tools like GitHub and coding workflows.  
- Overwhelmed by the need to quickly learn AI concepts and professional branding.  
- Unclear pathways into AI careers from non-technical fields.  

**Core Problem:** Seniors need structured, tailored onboarding and mentorship to bridge the gap between their current background and AI career goals.

## Goals

- Provide tailored onboarding workflows that highlight transferable skills.  
- Offer beginner-friendly resources for GitHub, AI literacy, and portfolio building.  
- Connect seniors to mentors and alumni who successfully transitioned.  
- Ensure compliance with accessibility, SEO, and GDPR standards for professional credibility.  
- Help seniors graduate with portfolio-ready assets and clear career pathways.  

## Success Metrics

- % of seniors completing onboarding and setting up LinkedIn, GitHub, portfolio, and Calendly.  
- Mentorship connections established through Discord or CRM.  
- Attendance at career-switching workshops and events.  
- Portfolio submissions demonstrating AI literacy.  
- Post-graduation surveys showing successful career transitions.
