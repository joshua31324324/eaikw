# SEO Strategy

This SEO strategy is built using three key resources:

- [Google SEO Starter Guide](https://developers.google.com/search/docs/fundamentals/seo-starter-guide)  
- [Google Search Essentials](https://developers.google.com/search/docs/essentials)  
- [100% Perfection Checklist](https://github.com/joshua31324324/eaikw/blob/main/SEO_QUICK_WIN_COMPLETE.md)  

---

## 1. Technical Foundations

- Ensure **crawlability**:
  - Robots.txt configured correctly.
  - XML sitemap submitted to Google Search Console.
- Maintain **indexability**:
  - Use canonical tags to prevent duplicate content.
  - Avoid noindex on important pages.
- Optimize **site performance**:
  - Fast load times (Core Web Vitals).
  - Mobile-first responsive design.
  - HTTPS enabled across all pages.
- Structured data:
  - Implement schema.org markup for products, articles, breadcrumbs, etc.
  - Validate with Google's Rich Results Test.

---

## 2. Content Strategy

- **High-quality, original content**:
  - Write for users first, search engines second.
  - Avoid thin or duplicate content.
- **Keyword optimization**:
  - Research intent-driven keywords.
  - Place keywords naturally in titles, headings, and body.
- **Content freshness**:
  - Regularly update evergreen pages.
  - Publish new content consistently.
- **Multimedia**:
  - Use descriptive alt text for images.
  - Optimize video and audio with transcripts/captions.

---

## 3. On-Page Optimization

- **Titles & Meta Descriptions**:
  - Unique, descriptive, keyword-rich.
  - Meta descriptions encourage click-through.
- **Headings (H1-H6)**:
  - Clear hierarchy, one H1 per page.
  - Use subheadings for scannability.
- **Internal linking**:
  - Logical structure with contextual anchor text.
  - Ensure important pages are linked frequently.
- **URL structure**:
  - Short, descriptive, keyword-friendly.
  - Avoid unnecessary parameters.

---

## 4. User Experience & Accessibility

- **Mobile usability**:
  - Responsive layouts.
  - Touch-friendly buttons and navigation.
- **Accessibility**:
  - WCAG 2.1 compliance (contrast, keyboard navigation, alt text).
  - Semantic HTML for screen readers.
- **Engagement signals**:
  - Clear CTAs.
  - Reduce bounce rate with relevant content.
- **Navigation**:
  - Consistent menus and breadcrumbs.
  - Search functionality for large sites.

---

## 5. Off-Page SEO

- **Backlinks**:
  - Earn high-quality links from authoritative sites.
  - Avoid manipulative link schemes.
- **Local SEO**:
  - Optimize Google Business Profile.
  - Consistent NAP (Name, Address, Phone) across directories.
- **Social signals**:
  - Share content across social platforms.
  - Encourage engagement and shares.

---

## 6. Compliance with Google Search Essentials

- **Content policies**:
  - No spammy practices (keyword stuffing, cloaking).
  - Avoid automatically generated low-quality content.
- **Behavior policies**:
  - No malicious behavior (phishing, malware).
  - Respect user privacy and safety.
- **Technical requirements**:
  - Ensure pages are accessible to Googlebot.
  - Provide accurate structured data.

---

## 7. Checklist Integration (Quick Wins)

From the **100% Perfection Checklist**:

- ✅ Titles under 60 characters, unique per page.  
- ✅ Meta descriptions under 160 characters, compelling.  
- ✅ H1 present and descriptive.  
- ✅ Alt text for all images.  
- ✅ Internal links to at least 3 other pages.  
- ✅ Page speed optimized (LCP < 2.5s, CLS < 0.1).  
- ✅ Mobile-friendly test passed.  
- ✅ Schema markup validated.  
- ✅ Robots.txt and sitemap tested.  
- ✅ No broken links or 404 errors.  

---

## 8. Monitoring & Continuous Improvement

- Track performance in **Google Search Console** and **Google Analytics**.  
- Monitor keyword rankings and adjust content strategy.  
- Regularly audit site for technical issues.  
- Update content based on user behavior and search trends.  

---

## Summary

This SEO strategy combines **Google's official guidelines** with a **practical checklist** to ensure:

- Technical excellence  
- High-quality content  
- User-first design  
- Compliance with search policies  
- Continuous optimization  

The result is a **future-proof SEO framework** that maximizes visibility, usability, and trust.
